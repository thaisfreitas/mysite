Current projects

  • Linguistic Magic (under construction). Khan Academy for language learning. Initial target language: Mandarin Chinese. Features:
    • Promotes correct mental models and language learning strategies
    • A novel way of learning characters that breaks them into components from the very beginning
    • Pronunciation training (especially of tones) from day 1, using High Variability Phonetic Training
    • Plenty of reading and listening input
    • Vocabulary is re-introduced in spaced repetition cycles so that learner sees vocabulary multiple times in different contexts
    • A mix of different activities so that the learner never gets bored
    • Tools used: Flask, Elm, PostgreSQL, JavaScript
  • Writing advice advice (data collection stage)
    • Empirically determine whether writing advice (e.g. adverbs are evil) actually works, using a database of fan fiction.
    • Also explore other factors that make a story more or less popular in fandom (e.g. are slash stories better reviewed?)
    • Tools used: requests, BeautifulSoup, Pandas, R, scikit-learn, PyMC3, NLTK, TextBlob, pattern.en, spaCy, gensim
